About Renit

Renit provides a medium to people who don’t have access to adequate resources and to those who have the said resources underutilized. It enables the creators, the artists, the entrepreneurs or any individual with a wish but unabundant resources to fulfil their inherent desires by renting on Renit and simultaneously save the environment.

Our Mission

Renit Incorporation was established with a mission to help individuals make affordable consumption decisions as well as to enable users to employ their underutilized or additional resources..
